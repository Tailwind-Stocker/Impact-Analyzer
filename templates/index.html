What this means?

Positive Score

The positive scoring functionality enables you to assess the level of positivity in your text, measuring its overall uplifting effect. It is derived from a comprehensive database containing more than 2000 positive words. A higher score signifies a stronger degree of positivity and impact within your content.

Negative Score

The negative score functionality aids in assessing the negativity within your text, revealing its adverse impact. It is calculated based on a comprehensive list of over 2000 negative words. Higher the score, the more negative the content's effect is perceived to be.

Polarity Score

The polarity score functions as a gauge of the general sentiment conveyed in a text. It is calculated using precise equations and varies between -1 and 1. A more negative score implies a greater presence of negative sentiment in the text, whereas a more positive score signifies a higher prevalence of positive sentiment. A text that is well-balanced and neutral usually falls near zero on the polarity spectrum.
